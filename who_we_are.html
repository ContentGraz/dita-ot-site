DITA Open Toolkit is maintained and developed by a team of volunteer developers.

The toolkit was originally developed within IBM and was open sourced in 2005. Since that time, DITA-OT has been updated significantly by a various companies and individuals.

Core Committers

Robert D Anderson

Robert is the project lead and core developer of DITA-OT, representing IBM. He's also the co-editor of the OASIS DITA specification.

Jarno Elovirta

Jarno is the lead developer of DITA-OT, working on the project as an individual contributor.
